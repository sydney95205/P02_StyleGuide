About FUKU

FUKU is a lifestyle retailer dedicated to inspiring customers through a unique combination of product, creativity and cultural understanding. Founded in 1800 in a small space across the street from the University of Simon Fraser University, FUKU now operates over 20 stores in the United States, Canada and Europe, offering experiential retail environments and a well-curated mix of on-trend women’s and men’s clothes, from boho dresses, denim. FUKU also carries everything you need to decorate your small space, apartment or dorm. Our Socks collection is a go-to destination for socks accessories. Visit our stores, where all are welcome and there’s always something new to see, or shop online at FUKU.com.
